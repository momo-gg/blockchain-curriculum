# Appendix B: Blockchain & Solana Timeline

A chronological overview of key events in blockchain history and Solana's development.

> ⚠️ **Currency Note**: Market caps, TVL figures, and token prices mentioned in this timeline are historical snapshots and do not reflect current values. Blockchain markets are highly volatile.

---

## Pre-Blockchain Era

### 1991
- **Stuart Haber and W. Scott Stornetta** publish first work on cryptographically secured chain of blocks

### 1997
- **Adam Back** invents Hashcash, proof-of-work system (later used in Bitcoin)

### 1998
- **Wei Dai** proposes "b-money," an anonymous distributed electronic cash system
- **Nick Szabo** designs "bit gold," a decentralized digital currency mechanism

### 2004
- **Hal Finney** creates Reusable Proof of Work (RPOW)

---

## Bitcoin Era (2008-2015)

### 2008
- **October 31**: Satoshi Nakamoto publishes Bitcoin whitepaper "Bitcoin: A Peer-to-Peer Electronic Cash System"

### 2009
- **January 3**: Bitcoin genesis block mined
- **January 12**: First Bitcoin transaction (Satoshi to Hal Finney)

### 2010
- **May 22**: First real-world Bitcoin transaction (10,000 BTC for two pizzas)
- **July**: Mt. Gox exchange launches

### 2011
- Other cryptocurrencies emerge (Litecoin, Namecoin)
- Silk Road marketplace launched (later shut down)

### 2013
- **December**: Vitalik Buterin publishes Ethereum whitepaper

### 2014
- Ethereum crowdsale raises ~$18M
- Mt. Gox collapse (850,000 BTC lost)

### 2015
- **July 30**: Ethereum mainnet launches with "Frontier" release
- First smart contracts deployed

---

## Smart Contract Era (2016-2019)

### 2016
- **The DAO** raises $150M, later hacked for $60M
- Ethereum hard fork creates Ethereum Classic
- First major ICO boom begins

### 2017
- ICO mania peaks (EOS, Filecoin, Tezos raise billions)
- **December**: Bitcoin reaches ~$20,000
- CryptoKitties congests Ethereum network
- **November**: Anatoly Yakovenko begins work on Solana, writes first Proof of History whitepaper

### 2018
- Market crash ("crypto winter")
- **February**: Solana Labs founded by Anatoly Yakovenko and Raj Gokal
- Stablecoins gain traction (USDC, DAI)

### 2019
- "DeFi Summer" precursors emerge
- Compound, Uniswap v1, Maker gain adoption
- **March**: Solana testnet launches
- **July**: Solana raises $20M in Series A

---

## Solana Development (2020)

### Q1 2020
- **March 16**: Solana mainnet beta launches
- Initial validators join network
- First programs deployed

### Q2 2020
- Serum DEX announced (FTX/Alameda partnership)
- Wormhole bridge development begins
- DeFi protocols start building on Solana

### Q3 2020
- **August**: Serum launches on mainnet
- Raydium development begins
- Solana Foundation grants program launches

### Q4 2020
- **November**: Solana reaches $1 billion TVL
- Ecosystem hackathon attracts developers
- Token program gains adoption

---

## Growth Era (2021)

### Q1 2021
- **January**: SOL price begins significant rise
- Phantom wallet launches
- Mango Markets launches

### Q2 2021
- **April**: Metaplex NFT standard introduced
- **May**: First major NFT collections launch
- Marinade Finance (liquid staking) launches
- SOL reaches all-time high of ~$55

### Q3 2021
- **August**: Degenerate Ape Academy mint (NFT mania begins)
- **September 14**: Network outage (17 hours) due to bot activity
- Solana reaches $200 billion market cap
- Magic Eden NFT marketplace launches

### Q4 2021
- **November 6**: SOL reaches all-time high of ~$260
- Breakpoint conference in Lisbon
- Solana Ventures announces $100M gaming fund
- Major institutional investment

---

## Challenges & Maturity (2022)

### Q1 2022
- **January 21**: Network degradation (bots, congestion)
- **February 2**: Wormhole exploit ($320M, later recovered)
- Priority fees mechanism improved
- Stake-weighted QoS implemented

### Q2 2022
- **April-May**: Network stability issues continue
- Terra/Luna collapse impacts broader crypto market
- **June**: Optimizations for transaction processing
- Saga phone announced

### Q3 2022
- **August**: Slope wallet exploit ($4M)
- State compression (compressed NFTs) announced
- Network stability improves
- Firedancer development announced

### Q4 2022
- **November**: FTX collapse
- SOL drops to ~$8
- Despite market conditions, development continues
- Grizzlython hackathon launches

---

## Recovery & Innovation (2023)

### Q1 2023
- Token-2022 (Token Extensions) launched
- Compressed NFT minting becomes available
- Jito MEV client gains adoption
- Network processes 20M+ daily transactions

### Q2 2023
- **April**: Saga phone ships
- xNFT (executable NFTs) launched with Backpack
- State compression reduces NFT costs 1000x
- DeFi TVL begins recovery

### Q3 2023
- **August**: Token Extensions adoption grows
- Firedancer development milestones
- Network achieves consistent uptime
- Breakpoint conference in Amsterdam

### Q4 2023
- SOL price recovers significantly
- **December**: Solana outperforms major cryptocurrencies
- DePIN (Decentralized Physical Infrastructure) narrative emerges
- Helium migrates to Solana
