# Appendix B: Blockchain & Solana Timeline

A concise, source-first timeline. Use the references below for exact dates, amounts, and release milestones (as of each source).

---

## Key Eras (High Level)

### Early Cryptography (Pre-2008)
- Foundational ideas in digital cash and cryptographic timestamping emerge.

### Bitcoin Era (2008–2015)
- Bitcoin whitepaper, genesis, and early network growth.
- First major exchanges and early ecosystem milestones.

### Smart Contract Era (2015–2019)
- Ethereum mainnet launches and the first wave of smart contracts and DeFi.
- ICO boom, followed by consolidation.

### Solana Era (2020–Present)
- Solana mainnet beta launches.
- Ecosystem growth across DeFi, NFTs, and consumer apps.
- Multiple validator clients emerge; performance and reliability initiatives continue.

---

## Anchor Milestones (No Exact Dates)

- Bitcoin whitepaper and genesis launch
- Ethereum mainnet launch and early DeFi
- Solana mainnet beta launch
- Rise of NFTs as a consumer on-chain use case
- Multiple validator clients (Agave + Firedancer)
- Ongoing reliability upgrades and incident response processes
